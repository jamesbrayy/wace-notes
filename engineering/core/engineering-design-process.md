# engineering design process

***

## summary
* the engineering design process is a systematic approach employed by engineers to develop solutions to problems or create new products.
* the engineering design process is crucial for effective problem-solving, innovation, risk management, communication, and a project's overall success
    * it provides a systematic framework from which to navigate a problem

## four key stages:
1. investigate
    * develop a comprehensive design brief
    * identify and assess existing solutions
    * research and critique components and materials
    * assess sources of energy
2. devise
    * produce pictorial and orthographic projections
    * compare, analyse, and justify the final choice of design
3. produce
    * present specifications for the selected solution
    * develop a timeline
    * construct and test solutions as you go
4. evaluate
    * evaluate whether it meets the requirements of the design brief
    * whether it is safe, functional, and well-finished
    * whether there were modifications and changes to the design during production
